**Treasurer’s Report**

Joan presented a Treasurer’s report. Finances are in good order. We have 22k$ in checking and $66K in reserve for future dock projects. We receive 20k$/yr in dues and can save 8-10k$ year.

2-4 k$ materials expense for dock repair is expected. Volunteer labor supports dock repair.

Transfer fees were implemented last year at $1000 per lot. We have received $4k as of now.  This is similar to other HOA transfer fees.

We have a goal of 10 years additional life on the docks. If they were to fail today, we would need $400K to cover replacement, necessitating an assessment from the membership. At this point no assessment is anticipated.

**Mail Boxes**

There are no major issues with the Mail Boxes at this time.

Some boxes need oiling with WD 40 to resolve sticking issue

**Docks and Common Area**

Older wood shows a better life. Wood available for repairs today has a shorter life.

The Board suggested replacement of the 12 ft ramp with a 16 ft wide ramp with elimination of the second ramp. The current ramp is in disrepair and is too small. It needs to be widened

Dave obtained an estimate of $19k last year. We propose getting 3 new estimates

and communicating with membership via email. The project will be progressed after the current season.

Pete wondered how many supported the proposal. A vote was held and resulted in a 12-4 majority in favor of progressing the project.

**New Business**

Dale proposed kayak storage facilities in the common area. Dave will follow up and collect info on a possible project.

A reminder to the members that they must pick-up after their dogs.

We need volunteers to sign up for gardening at the entrance. Cathy has been doing the work but needs some help.  Please contact Cathy (703-731-7166) if you are interested.

One resident has a tree falling across her property line. Pete suggested that the County has pervue in this area.
